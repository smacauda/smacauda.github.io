About

I have a background in experimental particle physics working with the CMS Collaboration. During my time with the collaboration, I worked on the Forward Pixel Upgrade (FPix) of the silicon tracker detector. My research was conducted primarily at UIC and Fermilab in IL. Following graduation, I spent 3 months at CERN to complete testing of the pixel detector modules.

Since leaving the collaboration, I have been focusing on learning everything I can about data science through online courseware and personal projects.
I am currently working as a tutor and freelance data analyst part-time.
